# **App Name**: Resolution Bingo Team

## Core Features:

- User Authentication: Register, login, and manage user profiles with email verification.
- Team Creation and Invitation: Create a team, invite users, and manage team settings for New Year's Resolution Bingo.
- Resolution Management: Add, update, and delete personal resolutions, and define team resolutions as a team leader.
- Team Bingo Setup: Create team bingo cards, including generating resolutions for others
- AI Bingo Card Generator: Upon starting the teambingo and based on team resolutions and the team member resolution proposals, an AI tool will automatically generate bingo cards for each team member.
- Resolution Approval and Verification: Upload proof for completed resolutions and manage the approval process within the team.
- Bingo Card Interaction: Users mark resolutions as 'completed' or 'to complete' on their bingo cards and view other users' progress in real-time.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Saturated purple (#A050BE) to give a creative and inviting feel.
- Background color: Very light desaturated purple (#F2E8F7) as a soft and clean base.
- Accent color: Analogous pink (#BE507F), significantly brighter than the primary to give highlight actions a punch.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' sans-serif for headings and 'Inter' sans-serif for body text.
- Use flat, geometric icons related to goal setting and celebration for a modern look.
- Clean and well-spaced layout, emphasizing readability and ease of navigation.
- Subtle animations for feedback on user actions (e.g., marking a resolution as complete).
